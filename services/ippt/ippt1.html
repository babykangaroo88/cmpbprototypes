Book your IPPT

You can take the 3-station Pre-Enlistee IPPT every Wednesday (except public holidays) at Toa Payoh Stadium at 7.30 am or 8.00 am

You must make the booking by Tuesday noon in order to take the IPPT test on Wednesday.

The 3 stations are

  • Sit-ups
  • Push-ups
  • 2.4km run

Booking takes around 3 minutes.
